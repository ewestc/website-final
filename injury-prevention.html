Injury prevention for runners

Sleep, eat, rest, stretch.


Click on a card to learn about injury prevention tips!

  • Mileage
    Don't run too much too soon, or put yourself at risk of getting an injury.
  • Ice Ice Baby
    Ice for 15 minutes after every long run. For shorter runs, you do not need to ice.
  • Stretch
    Stretch before AND after your runs! Stick with dynamic stretches before runs and static stretches after runs.
  • Cross-train
    Especially if it is strength training. I like to weight lift or cycle.
